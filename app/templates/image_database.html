CANCERAI is trained on the BreakHis dataset — one of the most widely used benchmark datasets for breast cancer histopathology image classification.

BreakHis v1 Dataset

The Breast Cancer Histopathological Image Classification (BreakHis) dataset was collected from 82 patients at the P&D Laboratory, Brazil. It contains 7,909 microscopic images of breast tumor tissue stained with Hematoxylin and Eosin (H&E) at four magnification factors.
