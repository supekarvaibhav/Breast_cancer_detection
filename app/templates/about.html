About CANCERAI

A research-grade AI platform for early breast cancer detection using a CNN+SVM hybrid model trained on the BreakHis histopathology dataset, combined with a real-time doctor consultation system.

Our Mission

Breast cancer is the most common cancer in women worldwide. Early detection can increase the 5-year survival rate to over 99%. CANCERAI aims to democratize access to early detection by providing an AI-powered screening tool available to both patients and clinicians.
